What's GeoHappening?

Socialize at your local event or conference (ahem...Dev Summit) with this real time social mapping app.

Chat with fellow map nerds, holler if you are at a good presentation, or just find nearby awesomeness.

Pan around the map to see where people are talking (all messages are geolocated) or just click on a message in Happenings to zoom to that location.

Check out our search page as well. Super neat auto complete enabled search query on all message history allowing easy search for specific topics.

The JavaScripts:

  • jQuery
  • Underscore.js
  • Backbone.js
  • Twitter Bootstrap
  • Typeahead.js
  • Firebase.js
  • Modernizr
  • And of course...ESRI JavaScript API (Dojo)
Developed by Jeremy Folds and Nick Volpe in Fort Collins, CO
